Subject:          Issuance of Computerized Extracts from the Register of Periodic Records-of-Rights (Fards) in Lahore District.

Dear Sir,

It is appreciable that record of land revenue is being computerized and issuance of computerized Fards has been started in Lahore District.

The issuance of Computerized Fards from Kiosks still needs the intervention of Patwaris which causes double trouble for the landowners.

Kiosks do not issue Fards without having picture and thumb impression of landowner. In case landowner is incapable to visit the Kiosk due to genuine reasons, such as being sick person or highly busy person, how he can obtain Fard? There is no specific touchstone to meet such situation which needs immediate attention to facilitate general public.

You are requested to please take up the matter on urgent basis as one of my client has already been victimized due to this process. He could not come to Kiosk due to his illness, and during the consultation with Revenue Authorities, he died without having Fard for Gift which has prevented him to dispose of his property at his own will.

Despite the issuance of Fards, why not the access of computerized fards is given to the Sub-Registrars concerned in Lahore to get rid from the issuance of fards? It will prevent the unnecessary visitation of landowners, time wastage, and money, too.

Problem may be overcome with the arrangement of mobile unit as NADRA has mobile unit which goes to the door steps of the general public for the supply of Identity Cards.

Hope the matter will be taken up to resolve the genuine problems expeditiously.

 

Yours faithfully,
