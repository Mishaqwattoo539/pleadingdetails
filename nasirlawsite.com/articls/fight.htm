Fight against America

When Iraq imposes war against Kuwait, entire world permits UNO forces to take action against Iraq. When America is surrounded into troubles while internal disintegration, world permits her to fight against so-called terrorism, i.e., Afghanistan. But when America opens war Iraq without lawful justification and permission of UNO, why UNO gets silent on its bloody act? Why UNO does not allow its forces to fight war against America? Why the offensive silence on such aggression is carried on? Where the civilization has been lost? Why the Butcher has been set at liberty? Why the Iraq has been made slaughter house? Why the American assets are not freezed to avoid war?

March 26, 2003
