Drive in Zoo – A big fraud

First of all park administration has played a big fraud to general public in the name of “Drive in Zoo” in Jallo Park, Lahore, as there was no recreation as was propagated in different commercials. It was held from March 27 to 29, 2004. Expect few aquariums, a monkey juggling, and a singing woman, there was nothing to enjoy, especially for those who came from far away of Lahore. There were no colorful programs as propagated.

Secondly, the portion of the entry ticket pass which was issued was the part which is retained with the book. Copies of the two tickets issued were sent to Jallo Administration in token of proof the maladministration on the part of Jallo Park Administration, but no action has been taken so far. Who is responsible for such illegality and inconvenience? Would the administration take appropriate punitive action against black marketers? Would the administration like to clarify the mislead commercials for special event which lead the public to visit the park but found nothing?

Sunday March 28, 2004
