Demolition of Bridge

Bridges are made to facilitate travelers which reduce the cost and time consumption. Construction involves heavy budgets/costs. Bridge on canal between Muslim Town, Lahore to Punjab University has been demolished by spending huge cost. Justification advanced is that it had became now redundant after construction of underpass at Muslim Town. What a good justification! Allah give them wisdom! This is not a national service, however it is wastage of national resources. People have to take long turn particularly who come for morning walk. It is totally unwarranted work.

Tuesday April 27, 2003
