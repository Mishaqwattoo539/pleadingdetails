Last Updated: Thursday September 11, 2003

Delay in result

Examination of LL. B. – III was held from August 26, 2002 to September 16, 2002 under Punjab University. More than five months have been elapsed but result is still awaited. Students are under great stress. It has also put students in uncertainity. Holding of supplementary examination shall take further time. Enrollment test is also going to be held on March 08, 2003. Punuab Bar Council is another dilemma which has also caused mental agony by not providing complete information for enrolment test. Responsibles of Punjab University are requested to declare result of LL. B. – III immediately preventing the disappointment and suffering of students. It will enable them to make future plans. These lines are written without any prejudice or ill will.

February 22, 2003
