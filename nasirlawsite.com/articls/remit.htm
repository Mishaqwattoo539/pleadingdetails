GOVERNMENT OF THE PUNJAB

REVENUE DEPARTMENT

 

Lahore dated 4th September, 2000

NOTIFICATION

No.2778-2000/1311 ST(I). In exercise of the powers conferred on hi under Section 33(2) of the Cooperative Societies’ Act, 1925 (Sind Act VII of 1925) and in suppression of all previous notifications on the subject, the Governor of the Punjab is pleased to remit the Stamp Duty on transfer of proprietary rights by the Cooperative Housing Societies to its members.

IMTIAZ MASRUR

SECRETARY TO GOVERNMENT OF THE PUNJAB
