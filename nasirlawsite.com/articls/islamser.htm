Service for Islam

We have been irritated by the repeated news from medial that President Prevaiz Musarraf is doing well being servant of Islam. We are unable to understand with respect of his services toward Islam being alleged.

If we are not wrong, collateral losses in Afghanistan, Iraq, and Pakistan are good examples which prove otherwise. Our cease fire has provided an opportunity for India to fence its border toward Kashmir to stop, so called, infiltration. American Foreign Minister along-with Indian counterpart has welcomed the initiatives taken by President Pervaiz Musharraf to facilitate them in arriving on the conclusion where they may divert the struggle of Kashmiri People to whom they call terrorists.

Don’t forget that what impression should be taken from the words of those who do not want to see us as we want. When our enemies pay tribute to our president, what it implies? They are happy as their work has become easy, which they could not do since the creation of Pakistan. How our enemy deals us in such a way that we quote its examples!

If anybody could help me regarding the services of our President Pervaiz Musharraf, I shall be highly thankful to him. I, alone, could not perceive his services rendered. I have perceived him as natural calamity to Islam and Muslims.

Thursday January 22, 2004
