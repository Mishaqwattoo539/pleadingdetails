OBITUARY

It is announced with profound grief and sorrow that UNO a sister of Commonwealth has been killed on March 20, 2003. America had brutally wounded her. She was 55 years old. She left behind 199 states as survivors. She was an orphan right from her birth. Now world has turned into orphan. The vacuum which has been created shall remain unfilled. Demised UNO was ill treated through out her life span.

She was born after the death of Commonwealth. She was co-guardian of its entire member states. She passed her youth in sickness. One of her rivals put her in a cage, wounded fatally and she succumbed to her injuries.

She failed to train her member as her successors. She also could not enjoy her life well at all. Her life was full of grieves and sorrows. She could not grow as required. Many times she miraculously survived after came back from the point of death. She could not survive at length and died before completion of her natural life. She tried to give peace and happiness to world but remained unsuccessful. Alas! She could not survive and died presently an immature death.

April 19, 2003
