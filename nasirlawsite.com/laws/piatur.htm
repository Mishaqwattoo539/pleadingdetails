The Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension of Trade Unions and Existing Agreements) Order (Repeal) Act, 2008

ACT II OF 2008

 

An Act to repeal the Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension of Trade Unions and Existing Agreements) Order 2001

Whereas it is expedient to repeal the Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension of Trade Unions and Existing Agreements) Order, 2001 (C.E"s Order No. 6 of 2001);

It is hereby enacted as follows:---

1. Short title and commencement.-(1) This Act may be called the Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension of Trade Unions and Existing Agreements) Order (Repeal) Act, 2008.

(2) It shall come into force at once.

2. Repeal of the Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension  of  Trade  Unions  and  Existing  Agreements)  Order, 2001 (C.E’s Order No. 6 or 2001).-(1) The Pakistan International Airline Corporation (Suspension of Trade Unions and Existing Agreements) Order 2001, (C.E’s Order No. 6 of 2001) is hereby repealed:---

Provided that the trade unions, associations, organizations or groups of any type of employees suspended under the aforesaid Order shall be registered afresh in accordance with law.

(2) Notwithstanding the repeal of the C. E’s Order No. 6 of 2001, and without prejudice to the provisions of sections 6 and 24 of the General Clauses Act, 1897 (X of 1897), all administrative orders issued, actions taken and modifications made in settlements shall continue in force until altered or rescinded.
