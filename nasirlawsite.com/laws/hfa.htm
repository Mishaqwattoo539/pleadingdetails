Preamble.

WHEREAS it is expedient to consolidate and amend the law relating to reserved forests and waste-lands in the Hazara District, and whereas the previous sanction of the Governor-General under sub-section (3) of section 80- A. of the Government of India has been obtained;

It is hereby enacted as follows:---
