The Validation of Laws Act, 1975

1ACT No. LXIII OF 1975

An Act to validate certain President's Orders, Martial Law Regulations, Martial Law Orders and other laws

[24th July 1975]

WHEREAS clause (1) of Article 270 of the Constitution of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan provides that Parliament may by law validate all Procla­mations, President's Orders, Martial Law Regulations, Martial Law Orders and other laws made between the twenty-fifth day of March 1969, and the nine­teenth day of December 1971;

AND WHEREAS it is expedient to validate certain President's Orders, Martial Law Regulations, Martial Law Orders and other laws made as­aforesaid;

It is hereby enacted as follows:---

1. Short title and commencement.-(1) This Act may be called the Valida­tion of Laws Act, 1975.

(2) It shall come into force at once.

2. Validation of laws. The laws mentioned in the Schedule are hereby declared to have been validly made by competent authority.
