1.       That the deceased above named was died intestate.

2.       That the petitioners are the only lawful issues/survivors of the deceased, thus entitled for the succession from the estate of their deceased father. Being successor, they have vested right for the grant of Succession Certificate.

3.       That after the death of Asif Mehmood Butt on January 19, 1997, the father of petitioners, Mst. Aasia Butt, the mother of petitioners also died on March 02, 1998, before claiming her hereditary rights in her life, so claimed in the instant application.

4.       That Guardian of minors applied for the grant of legal dues of Asif Mehmood Butt, deceased, from Pakistan Telecommunication Limited, but they denied, vide their letter dated January 23, 2003, requiring the further orders from the Guardian Judge and Succession Certificate for the payment of dues.

5.       That the value of legal dues, i.e., Benevolent Fund and Group Insurance etc. is not ascertainable as department of the deceased does not convey its value unless the Succession Certificate is produced to them.

6.       That all the petitioners hereby declare and affirm their willingness to grant a single Succession Certificate in the name of Najam-ud-Din S/o. Abdul Aziz, the Guardian of the minors in the instant petition.
