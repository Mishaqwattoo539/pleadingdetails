While the cause lists of the High Courts and Supreme Courts are published online on their respective websites, the district judiciary’s cause list is displayed outside the particular court, on the date of hearing. It can be handwritten or typed and will show you at what serial number your case is listed and when it would be called in court. Apart from the serial wise listing and title you can gather some other information too from these cause lists including case number, nature of case, and proceedings fixed for that day.
