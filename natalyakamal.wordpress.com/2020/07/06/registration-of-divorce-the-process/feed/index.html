Hi Saima. Are you based in Islamabad? Yes you take the certificate to NADRA who then changes your status etc and name. The record from Arbitration Council goes to NADRA once in two or three weeks to be uploaded manually. It is not automatic at least not in Islamabad. So sometime it will take two three weeks for NADRA system to show the certificate. If your husband has not given a fake (highly unlikely) then it is just upload issue and you can check with relevant Arbitration Council.
